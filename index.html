I've done some warm-ups to find something compelling for the Hubway Visualization Challenge. Each asks a question and tries to find an answer, and builds on previous experiments.
  1. Q: Where are the Hubway stations? Answer with a map
  2. Q: How are the start/end pairs distributed throughout the rides? Answer with an interactive map
  3. Q: Which stations are sources of rides and which are sinks? Answer by putting bars over each station
