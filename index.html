I've done some warm-ups to find something compelling for the Hubway Visualization Challenge. Each asks a question and tries to find an answer, and builds on previous experiments.
  1. Q: Where are the Hubway stations?
  2. Q: How are the start/end pairs distributed throughout the rides?
  3. Q: Which stations are sources of rides and which are sinks?
  4. Q: Which neighborhoods can we expect to actually see a Hubway bike moving about?
